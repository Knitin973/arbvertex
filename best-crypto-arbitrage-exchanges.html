Frequently Asked Questions

Which exchange is best for crypto arbitrage beginners?
Binance + Bybit together. Binance gives deepest liquidity and most pairs; Bybit has the lowest maker fee (0.01%) and best futures support.
Which exchange has the lowest fees?
MEXC — zero maker, 0.01% taker on futures. Works best for trades under $5,000 where thin 2026liquidity isn't a problem.
Do I need KYC to start arbitrage?
Bybit and MEXC allow limited trading without KYC. For serious arbitrage, complete KYC on all exchanges to avoid withdrawal limits mid-trade.
How much capital do I need to start?
As little as $100 per exchange. $100 on Binance + $100 on Bybit is enough to act on most ArbVertex signals from day one.
